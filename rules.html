üéØ Objective

Roll doubles (two dice showing the same number) to win the game! But beware of various challenges and opportunities along the way.

üé≤ Basic Gameplay

  • You start with 1 Protection Throw
  • Click "Roll Dice" to roll both dice simultaneously
  • If you roll doubles, you win immediately!
  • If you don't roll doubles, different outcomes can occur based on your roll

üéØ Lucky Number System

  • At the start of each game, choose your lucky sum (3 or 5)
  • If your dice add up to your lucky number during gameplay, you activate the Prediction Challenge!
  • In the Prediction Challenge, you predict what a single die will roll
  • Correct prediction = instant win!
  • Wrong prediction = return to normal gameplay (no bonus protection throw)
  • The prediction die automatically resets after each use

üõ°Ô∏è Protection Throws

  • You start with 1 Protection Throw
  • When you roll two even numbers (2,4,6), you gain an additional Protection Throw
  • If you don't roll doubles, you use a Protection Throw to continue playing
  • When you run out of Protection Throws, you're eliminated
  • Protection throws can also be earned through power-up re-rolls

‚ö° Power-up Throws

  • Triggered when you roll a 6 and an odd number (1,3,5)
  • You get to re-roll the odd-numbered die
  • If you roll a 6 on the re-roll, you win instantly!
  • If you roll an even number (2,4), you gain a Protection Throw
  • If you roll an odd number (1,3,5), you use a Protection Throw (or lose if none left)

üèÜ Winning Conditions

  • Natural Doubles: Roll two dice showing the same number (1/6 chance)
  • Power-up Doubles: Roll a 6 during a power-up re-roll
  • Prediction Win: Accurately predict the single die roll during Lucky Sum activation

üíÄ Losing Conditions

  • Run out of Protection Throws without rolling doubles
  • Fail a power-up throw when you have no Protection Throws left

üìä Statistics Tracking

  • Protection: Current Protection Throws remaining
  • Games: Total games played
  • Wins: Total games won
  • Lucky: Your chosen lucky sum number

üéÆ Game Flow Example

  1. Choose lucky number (3 or 5)
  2. Roll dice - hope for doubles!
  3. If sum equals lucky number ‚Üí Prediction Challenge
  4. If 6 + odd number ‚Üí Power-up opportunity
  5. If two even numbers ‚Üí Gain Protection Throw
  6. If no special conditions ‚Üí Use Protection Throw or lose
  7. Continue until you win or lose

üé≤ Dice Randomization

All dice rolls are completely randomized using JavaScript's Math.random() function, ensuring fair gameplay with equal probability for each outcome (1-6).
